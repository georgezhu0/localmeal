How localmeal works

By law, colleges and other institutions are required to throw away leftover food at the end of each business day. At the same time, there are families in need of food living in close range to these colleges. Localmeal works with colleges to schedule deliveries of this leftover food to families in need, eliminating food waste while bettering the community.
